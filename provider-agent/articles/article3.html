Beyond the Hype: NFTs and the Future of Digital Ownership

By Elena Kowalski, Digital Asset Researcher

Published: November 2025

NFTs Have Evolved

The NFT market has undergone significant transformation. While speculative trading has cooled, practical applications in gaming, identity, and intellectual property are flourishing.

Where Value is Being Created

  • Gaming assets with true ownership and interoperability
  • Digital identity and credential verification
  • Music and entertainment royalty distribution
  • Supply chain authentication

Investment Thesis

Look for projects building infrastructure rather than chasing collectible trends. The protocols enabling NFT utility will capture more value than individual collections.
